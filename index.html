About

FauDroids

FauDroids is a personal challenge where we develop one Android app every month in 2015 (think of it as a new years resolution). KeepOn was our app for July.

Find out more about FauDroids at http://faudroids.org our have a look at our other apps on the Google Play Store.

Also, everything about KeepOn is open source, so check it out on GitHub!

Contact

We love feedback!

We had fun building KeepOn and we want you to have fun using it. So if something is on your mind let us know, we want to hear from you!

You can reach us at ideas@faudroids.org, @faudroids or on GitHub. Also please consider heading over to the Playstore and rating KeepOn!
